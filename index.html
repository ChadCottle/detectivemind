Case #002: The Caesar Shift

Evidence: synt vf sha!

DEBRIEF: You identified a ROT13 shift. Because the English alphabet has 26 letters, shifting by 13 is a "self-inverse" operation. If you shift it again, you get your original message back!

DEBRIEF: You mastered Hexadecimal. Computer memory stores data in bytes. Hex is just a human-readable way to look at those bytes (Base 16). 48 in hex is 'H' in ASCII!
